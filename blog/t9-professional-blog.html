W9 Tech: Make Your Blog Professional

November 16, 2014

I'd never really been satisfied with the first version of this website, a snapshot of which I've included below:

I've always liked the way that some websites have made use of transparent effects and vector images. I tried to incorporate those things in my first website--as you can see from the above image--but I never thought I'd executed it all that well. This week, when DBC gave us an opportunity to revisit the design of our websites, I was psyched. I changed a lot of things (which you can see). I did more research on effective website layout, and I picked a color palette that was more conservative. I was finally able to implement changes based on critiques I'd received from other boots eariler in the Phase.

Overall, revamping my website was super fun. I'm happy with the results; I think the new site has a better overall format for presenting information. Also, by virture of implementing the new changes, I was able to give my site a better structure "under the hood." My stylesheets are arranged more sensibly (although arguably, they could probably be more DRY) & I used my tag IDs and Classes in a way that would allow me to make wide-sweeping changes to the site w/ relatively little effort. Who knows? Maybe with these changes, I'll redesign the site more often?
